About Me

Hi, I'm Amogh Koratpallikar, a senior at the University of Iowa's Tippie College of Business pursuing a BBA in Business Analytics and Information Systems. I have previous hands-on experience in data analysis and reporting, particularly in agricultural and financial sectors. I have worked two internships where I learned a broad range of skills including creating a PowerBI report for stakeholders, to assessing loan applications and analyzing financial statements to evaluate credit risk. I am always dedicated towards learning new things and exploring new opportunities.

My love for data began with analyzing fantasy football stats when I was younger. Comparing players and predicting outcomes sparked my curiosity about how numbers can drive better decisions. I continue to explore that passion by building solutions that turn raw data into meaningful insights.
